Class 12 Accountancy Overview

üßæ What is Accountancy?

Accountancy (or accounting) is the systematic process of recording, measuring, processing, and communicating financial transactions of a business or organization.

It serves to:

  • Track business performance (profit & loss)
  • Present financial position (assets, liabilities, equity)
  • Inform stakeholders (management, investors, regulators) for decision-making

üìö Why is Accountancy Used?

  • Decision Support: Helps management and investors make informed financial and operational decisions.
  • Compliance: Preparing statements following legal standards (e.g., Schedule III of Companies Act; GAAP).
  • Transparency & Trust: Provides credible financial info, enabling accountability to regulators, banks, and shareholders.
  • Performance Analysis: Tools like ratio analysis and cash flow statements offer deep insight into liquidity, profitability, and solvency.
