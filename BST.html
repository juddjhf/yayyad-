Class 12 Business Studies – Syllabus & Usage

🏢 What is Business Studies?

Business Studies explores management functions, business environment, finance, marketing, and consumer protection—preparing students to understand real-world business operations.

  • Unit IV – Planning

  • Unit VI – Staffing
  • Unit VII – Directing
  • Unit VIII – Controlling

Part B – Finance & Marketing

  • Unit IX – Financial Management
  • Unit X – Financial Markets
  • Unit XI – Marketing Management
  • Unit XII – Consumer Protection
